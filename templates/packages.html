PACKAGE 1

4 DAYS AND 3 NIGHTS

TRIVANDRUM | AZHIMALA SIVA TEMPLE | KOVALAM BEACH

AZHIMALA TEMPLE

DAY 01: ARRIVAL IN TRIVANDRUM

  • Arriving at Trivandrum Airport/station and you will be escorted to your hotel
  • After freshning up you will be contacted by our guide for sighseeing
  • Visit Napier Museum, Art gallery and kanakakunnu palace
  • A tour to Trivandrum Zoo followed by Lunch
  • Later in evening, visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Dinner at hotel and overnight stay

DAY 02: OFF TO AZHIMALA TEMPLE

  • Pick up at hotel by morning 5:00 am
  • Journey starts and reaches destination by about 6:15(approximate)
  • While returning a stop at Azhimala cliff and beach
  • After lunch a visit to Lulu mall for shopping
  • Returning to hotel by night
  • Dinner at hotel and overnight stay

DAY 03: OFF TO KOVALAM

  • Pick up at hotel by morning 7:30 am
  • Journey starts and ends by 8:30(aaproximate)
  • Visit kovalam art gallery and rock cave temple
  • After lunch a visit to halcyon castle at kovalam
  • At evening sightseeing in beaches(Kovalam Beach, Samudra Beach, Grove Beach)
  • Returning to hotel by 10:00 pm
  • Dinner at hotel and overnight stay

DAY 04: DEPARTURE

  • Pick up at hotel on next morning according to your flight/train reservations
  • Droping you at the airport/station for your onward journey back Home
